Bacterial phylogenetics in BEAST 2

View on GitHub

Bacter is a BEAST 2 package which facilitates joint Bayesian inference of ancestral recombination graphs (ARG) and related parameters from bacterial sequence alignments under the ClonalOrigin model.

ARGs

Bacter allows you to infer the ancestral recombination graph that gave rise to your sampled data.

Parameters

Bacter can infer parameters such as the recombination rate and expected converted tract length jointly with the ARG.

Population dynamics

Bacter includes support for jointly producing Bayesian Skyline Plot (BSP) population dynamics inferences.
