Bacterial phylogenetics in BEAST 2

View on GitHub

BACTER is a BEAST 2 package which facilitates inference of a (restricted kind of) ancestral recombination graph (ARG) and related parameters from a sequence alignment. It is based on the model described in Didelot et al.'s 2010 Genetics paper and is currently under heavy development. Check back soon!

bacter is maintained by Tim Vaughan.
